- ABOUT -

Connor Immonen is a voice actor based in Vancouver, Canada. While specializing in video games, he has also lent his voice to commercials and brought audiobooks to life, working with both local clients and international accounts.

Swinging large swords (and dodging them!) recreationally means that nothing fazes Connor and you can count on him to be flexible and responsive in his performances with the training to back up his naturally powerful voice.

Connor is also an accomplished writer and graphic illustrator. You can read his fantasy climate fiction series Hew and Cry.
